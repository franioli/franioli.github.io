Professional Summary

Post-doctoral researcher in Geomatics at Politecnico di Milano, specialised in photogrammetry and geoinformatics for environmental monitoring. My work bridges traditional photogrammetry and modern computer vision, with a focus on deep learning for image matching and automated 3D reconstruction pipelines, from low-cost terrestrial sensors to satellite multi-view stereo.
